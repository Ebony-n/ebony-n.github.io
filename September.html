My Favourite animals!

Author: Ebony N!

This article is going to be about some of my favourite animals!

Safari aniamals:

Girraffes, Hippos, Zebras, Elephants

Cold Climate animals:

My Favourite places to shop at!

Read time: 3 minutes

Author: Ebony N!

This article is going to be about some of my favourite places to shop at!

I dont usually like to shop but when I do these are the places I go to the most.

This is a list of some of my favourite places to shop in categories:

Shops in general:

Bookshops (Foyles, Waterstones)

Clothes shops:

Primark, H & M, Matalan

Grocery stores:

Sainsburys, Tesco, Asda, Lidl

Resteraunts/ Fast food:

Tortilla, Nandos (I don't buy food much outside)

Extras:

Superdrug, Boots

"I like going to bookshops!" - Ebony!, 2024

I don't go to many places. I prefer staying inside. I do like going to bookshops because they tend to be quiet and I can look at books that I may potentially buy to read. It is also a nice break from going into louder shops when clothes shopping or grocery shopping.
