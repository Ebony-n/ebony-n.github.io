My Favourite foods!

Read time: 3 minutes

Author: Ebony N!

This article is going to be about some of my favourite foods!

This is a list of some of my favourite foods in categories:

Main meals:

Pizza, Lasagna, Macaroni Cheese

Kenyan meals:

Ugali and Sukuma Wiki, Chapati, Mandazi

Fruit and Vegetables:

Strawberries, Grapes, Sweetcorn, Olives

Sweet treats:

Strawberry Pencils, Chocolates, Jaffa Cakes

"I love strawberries!" - Ebony!, 2024

Strawberries are my favourite fruit. I have always liked them. My phone screen is a strawberry. I remember always asking my mum to buy me strawberries because we never got to eat them often when I was younger. I also love strawberry flavoured milk. My favourite kind is the strawberry milkshake from Nesquik which I used to have all the time when I was younger.
