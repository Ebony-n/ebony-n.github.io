My Favourite animals!

Read time: 3 minutes

Author: Ebony N!

This article is going to be about some of my favourite animals!

This is a list of some of my favourite foods in categories:

Pets:

Rabbits, Cats, Guinea pigs, Hamsters

Safari aniamals:

Girraffes, Hippos, Zebras, Elephants

Cold Climate animals:

Penguins, Polar bears, Reindeers, Seals

Other mentions:

Dolphins, Peacocks, Flamingos, Cheetas, Sloths, Pandas, Lions (I like their mane)

"I love rabbits!" - Ebony!, 2024

Rabbits are my favourite animal. I like the fact that rabbits have soft fur. I also like the fact that they are intelligent.

I also like penguins. I chose to make a penguin doll when I was in year 7 in textiles class. I also loved watching the TV show Pingu when I was younger.
